DEPARTMENT OF YOGA
REPORT ON ACTIVITIES
(2020–21)


Regular Yoga classes were conducted online throughout the year for all the classes from std. I went to XII through online mode. International Day of Yoga was celebrated on 21st June 2020. On this day, a video was made on demonstrating Yoga practices. All students were made to practice during the first period on that day. Inter DAV Yoga competition was held on 31.03.2021.

The following students were trained by Mr. Kesavan and Mr. Kapil Sharma. They took part in the competition (both individual and group Events) which was held at the DAV Boys Sr. Sec. School, Mogappair.

Fit India Week , as initiated by the Government of India was also celebrated in a grand manner in our school.
The Physical Education Department teachers conducted online fitness sessions to teachers. Yoga demonstration video was done by Mr. Kesavan and the same was showed to all classes on 15.12.2020 and the children practiced them online.
The Yoga teachers, Mr. Kesavan and Mr. Kapil Sharma logged in to different classes to monitor the students doing the yoga poses.
Weekend Parent, Teachers and Alumni classes (online) were organised from September 2020.
Yoga teachers from all branches of DAV offered to take yoga classes. Following are the details in a nutshell.

Weekend classes were arranged for 5 weeks.
Every Saturday morning and Sunday morning one batch and Every Saturday evening and Sunday evening another batch would attend class.
From Boys Gopalapuram branch, Mr. Kesavan and Mr. Kapil Sharma conducted the classes.
Later, all the Yoga Teachers offered special classes on Yogic Management of different ailments.
Mr. Kesavan conducted Back Strengthening sessions on 31st October 2020 and 1st November 2020. Management of Insomnia sessions on 5th and 6th December 2020.
Mr. Kapil Sharma conducted Weight Loss program on 21st and 22nd November 2020. Relief from Arthritis sessions on 26th and 27th December 2020.
Offline Parent classes. Yoga classes were conducted for Parents in the school premises from 23rd January to 14th February 2021.
Totally 29 parents attended the sessions and got benefitted.
