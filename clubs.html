Eco Club

This academic year had been useful & informative for students inspite of the pandemic situation. The online platform provided by the school helped us to motivate the students in creating environmental awareness & also conducted various activities to conserve the environment. The above quote embodies the very purpose of quizzing.

As a part of Eco club, the students of class VIII made impressive posters for eco-friendly Diwali during the month of November 2020.This inculcated in them to keep the environment pollution free. They conveyed the message of CLEAN DIWALI-GREEN DIWALI and promoted the requirement of saving environment.

In the first week of December 2020, we have assigned a paper mache activity named as “Wealth out of waste” for class VI. The aim of this activity was to raise awareness among students regarding not to throw used things which can be used in different ways like decorative pieces, storage box, etc. All the students participated very enthusiastically and made beautiful decorative pieces, wall hangings, boxes with waste materials. This activity gave the message of reduce, reuse and recycle too. Hence the students enjoyed and learned the ways of conservation of nature, the importance of cleanliness of surroundings and minimizing the use of natural resources.

Computer Science Club


Computer science club was one of most active clubs since learning itself became online due to Pandemic. Computer Science club conducted activities for various classes based on various software learning in their CLP (Computer Literacy Programme).

The department conducted e-certificate designing competition for classes VI-XII ,where all classes participated in large number and best 3 was selected from each class ,The best of all was then selected for the certificate of merit to be awarded to the winners of Academic year 2019-20 .The certificate was designed by Tejaswin of Class VIII-C.


Quiz Club


"Knowledge has a beginning but no end”
The above quote embodies the very purpose of quizzing.

In the year 2018-2019, the DAV quiz club has displayed it’s excellence in various competitions held across the city as well as inside the campus and even on a state and national level.

The monthly sessions at the class room or in AV room, have given students an opportunity to prepare their own innovative quizzes. It has facilitated learning environment. The enthusiastic participation of students from different grades, showcases a very bright future for the Dav quiz club. The following are the key events our students have participated and won prizes .

Third consecutive time we have entered into the NDTV UL national safety science quiz at National level and won the 2nd runner up, our team bagged 3rd place in current affairs quiz organized by meenakshi sundarajan engineering college and won cash award of ₹10,000 and 2,000. All participants won gift voucher. Various inter school competition held across cities.

Particularly we have won overall championship and came first in quiz event conducted by NKT group of institution. First place in sparkle event conducted by vidhya mandir, and second place in maitree organized by chattinad vidhyashram, business quiz by State Bank of India etc… We conducted quiz on special day like independence day and Republic day and inculcate the value of knowledge apart from the textual material..

Science Club


Science Club activities kick started with the Quiz programme on 3rd December 2020 due to Covid disruption. The programme was conducted online by B.R.Subramaniam, alumni of DAV boys, Gopalapuram. Six teams and with two participants in each team participated with all enthusiasm in this quiz. The feedback from the participants was very positive on the outcome of the quiz.

A Guest Lecture online was arranged on 17 th December 2020 by Dr, Jaishri Ramakrishnan, Consultant therapist and counselor with three decades of experience in handling children. Due to lack of time the lecture touched upon issues associated with Adolescence and handling them. The boys of class VIII attended the lecture online.

As a part of Science club activity, poster making on COVID awareness is proposed for X standard students during third/ fourth week of January Project Display is proposed wherein the class IX students will apply the theoretical knowledge from classes and textbook , to make working models or concept charts in various science topics. Due to covid interruption, this activity is also planned as a virtual event.
