SCOUTS REPORT



Various achievements have been accomplished by our scouts team. A few of them include:

  • 10 scouts from our school, got the Rajyapuraskar award from the honourable Governor of Tamilnadu, which is the highest state award for the scout students. 10 Scouts were honoured with Merit certificate and Badge in the Rajyapuraskar Award 2018
  • Scouts were given the opportunity to participate in the Rajya Puraskar Exam
  • Scouts participated in the messenger of peace camp and were given an id to be a member of scout.org.
  • Fit India Cyclathon
